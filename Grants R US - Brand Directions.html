The name

For a firm asking agencies to trust you with public money, the name has to read as a peer of the institutions you serve. A shortlist, grouped by tone — pair any name with any direction below.

Candid note

“Grants R US” reads retail, not federal. It signals a discount toy-store cadence (“…R Us”) that undercuts a high-trust advisory pitch. It’s memorable, but it caps how premium the firm can feel. Recommendation: adopt a name that sounds like a partner a grants officer would shortlist. Strong leads are marked below.

Three logo directions

Each explores a different strategy, not just a different drawing — symbol-led, emblem-led, and pure wordmark. All in charcoal on white so structure carries the weight. Names shown are placeholders you can swap.

ASymbol-led · geometric mark

Meridian

A cartographic mark — globe with a single meridian — for a firm that helps applicants find the line and navigate the federal landscape. The symbol works alone as an app icon; the wordmark pairs a bold and light weight to feel considered, not corporate.

Keystone

An architectural emblem — the keystone of an arch, the one stone that lets the structure stand. The most overtly institutional route: it borrows the language of federal buildings without the cliché of an eagle or a seal. Reads as engineering and permanence.

CWordmark-led · typographic

Sterling

No symbol — the most modern, most minimal route. Confidence is shown by needing nothing but the name, set in letterspaced geometric caps with a single hairline rule. The “mark” is a typographic monogram in a thin frame. Scales effortlessly and never dates.

/ 01

Restraint signals trust

No gradients, no color, no ornament. The confidence to use only black, white, and space is what reads as premium and institutional.

/ 02

Structure is the brand

Hairline rules, mono labels, and a strict grid do the work. The identity should feel engineered — like the proposals it produces.

/ 03

One accent, held in reserve

Charcoal is the system. If a single accent is ever introduced, it stays disciplined — try one live in the Tweaks panel.
