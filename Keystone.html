How it works

A disciplined path from idea to award.

Every engagement runs the same structured sequence. You always know what stage you're in, what's due, and what's next.

01

Qualify

Confirm fit, eligibility, and the right solicitation. Honest go / no-go.

02

Strategize

Win themes, reviewer criteria, budget shape, and a writing plan.

03

Write

Narrative, work plan, budget, and every attachment — to spec.

04

Comply

Line-by-line audit against the solicitation and scoring rubric.

05

Submit

Package, submit, verify receipt — and prepare the next cycle.

Typical first cycle 6–10 weeks, depending on solicitation complexity and readiness.
